Expresso

Welcome to Expresso the mobile friendly point of sale system.

Perfect for popup stores, farmers markets or a carboot sale.

All you need is an internet connection and a way of taking payments such as Stripe.

No need for an expensive till, simply manage your sales through Expresso and a suitable banking app, all from your phone.

The default setup is for a small coffeeshop, edit it to build a POS system that works for you.

How To Use

Expresso has a card layout consisting of Menus, Submenus and Items.

Start by adding Menus, then Submenus and finally Items. Deleting a Menu will delete all associated Submenus and Items.

Deleting a Sub-Menu will delete all associated Items.

Items can be added to the current order which can be viewed in the Order tab from the navigation bar.

The current order total is displayed in the green button at the bottom of the order screen.

When payment has been taken, either cash or using a suitably enabled banking app, come back to the Order screen, tap/click the green button and the order will move to the Sales tab and the screen will clear ready for the next transaction.

Tap Sales to see the total number of orders that have been processed and their value. The sales for the day can be emailed as a .csv file that can be opened by Excel, Google Sheets and many other suitable applications.

Click/tap 'Menus' to get started
