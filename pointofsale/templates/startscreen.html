Expresso

Welcome to Expresso the mobile friendly point of sale system.

Perfect for a small coffeeshop or cafe. Great also for popup stores, farmers markets or even a carboot sale.

All you need is an internet connection and a way of taking payments such as Stripe.

No need for an expensive till, simply manage your sales through Expresso and a suitable banking app.

The default setup on this app is for a small cafe, edit it to build an app that works for you.

How To Use

Expresso runs a simple card based menu system consisting of Menus, Submenus and Items.

Start by adding Menus, then Submenus and finally Items. Deleting a Menu will delete all associated Submenus and Items.

Deleting a Sub-Menu will delete all associated Items.

Items can be added to the current order which can be viewed in the Order tab from the navigation bar.

Tap Sales to see the total number of orders that have been processed and their value.
