Welcome to Expresso the mobile friendly point of sale system.

Perfect for a small coffeeshop or cafe. Great also for popup stores, farmers markets or even a carboot sale.

All you need is an internet connection and a way of taking payments such as Stripe.

No need for an expensive till, simply manage your sales through Expresso and a suitable banking app.

The default setup on this app is for a small cafe, edit the Menus, Sub-Menus and Items to build an app that works for you.

Tap Order to see what items have been added to the bill.

Tap Sales to see a list of the day's transactions.
